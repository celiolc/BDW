    WHAT IS BDW?

    A brief description of what BDW is:



    BDW stands for Business Data Warehouse.
    The system is responsible by receiving Worldwide Procurement data and summarizing it in order to create an easy way to find all data related to IBM's products and services.

    Here you can find every main detail related to every transaction ever made at IBM®. The system contains several tables containing WorldWide Spend data, Products Items/Orders Summaries, Worldwide Travel Expenses and much more.
    Broadly speaking, BDW is a huge repository where you can find all you need about products and services in an easy way.
